Landmark of Heroes:
Honoring Courage and Legacy

History

The Landmark of Heroes is an artistic monument in Jones, Isabela, created by artist Joel Pragas. It commemorates the Battle of Payac, where native Cagayanos fought Spanish forces in 1787 — a lesser‑known yet significant local struggle for liberty. The monument also features busts of past local leaders.

Activities

Sightseeing, photography, and learning about local heroism through the sculptural details.

Best Time to Visit

Mornings or late afternoons for better light for photos and cooler temperatures.

How to Get There

From the town center of Jones, local transport or a short walk/ride will get you to the monument within the community.

Tips

Bring water, and approach respectfully since it honors historical figures.

A meaningful stop for those interested in local history and the heroes of Isabela.
