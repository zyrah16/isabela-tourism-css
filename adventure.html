Explore the Wild Side of Isabela

From waterfalls to forests and rivers, discover nature and adventure across the province.

Bonsai Forest

Discover the unique dwarf trees and scenic coastal views of Isabela’s Bonsai Forest.

Crocodile Sanctuary

Experience wildlife conservation and learn about crocodiles in their natural habitat.

Dibulo Falls

Trek through nature and be rewarded with the refreshing beauty of Dibulo Falls.

Dicotcotan Beach

Relax on golden sands and enjoy the peaceful coastal charm of Dicotcotan Beach.

Fuyot Springs

Unwind in cool spring waters surrounded by lush greenery and forest landscapes.

Abuan River

Experience the thrill and beauty of river activities along Abuan River.

Dimasilong Eco-Tourism Park

Enjoy scenic views, outdoor activities, and a refreshing escape at Dimasilong Park.

Bird Watching at Malasi Lake

Observe diverse bird species in the peaceful surroundings of Malasi Lake.

Kweba Danum

Explore hidden caves and underground wonders waiting to be discovered.

Whether you're chasing waterfalls or exploring hidden trails, Isabela’s natural beauty offers an adventure worth experiencing.
