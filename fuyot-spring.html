Fuyot Springs National Park:
A Natural Escape

About

A protected national park near Ilagan City featuring mountain trails, caves, waterfalls (including Pinzal Falls), springs, and forest landscapes. It’s a hub for nature lovers and adventurers.

Activities

Hiking, spelunking, birdwatching, ATV rides, zipline, natural pools and springs.

Best Time to Visit

Dry months for safer cave explorations and waterfall treks.

How to Get There

Located in Barangay Santa Victoria, Ilagan City — accessible by local transport from Ilagan town center.

Tips

Wear comfortable gear for trails and bring insect repellent — forests can be buggy.

From mountains to springs, every trail at Fuyot invites you to explore and connect with nature.
