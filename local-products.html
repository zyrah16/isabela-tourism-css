Isabela’s Treasures:
Local Products

Delicacies

Discover the flavors of Isabela through its local delicacies and specialty products. From the refreshing Gin-C drink made with turmeric and calamansi to mung bean products from San Mateo, each treat reflects local creativity and tradition. Enjoy Big Brother Cornick from Cauayan City and the unique taste of Pinilisa, a rare purple rice with a soft texture and nutty aroma.

Crafts

Discover Isabela’s craftsmanship through locally made products that reflect tradition and creativity. From woven Sabutan handicrafts in Palanan to traditional Banga pottery, each piece carries cultural meaning. Experience the intricate Inabel textiles and the comfort of handcrafted Butaka chairs—showcasing skill, artistry, and heritage passed down through generations.

Support local, discover unique crafts, and take home a piece of Isabela with you.
