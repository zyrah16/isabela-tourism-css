Kweba Danum:
Into the Depths of Nature

About

A hidden cave inside the Tumauini Watershed Natural Park, Kweba Danum’s name means “water” in Ilokano, reflecting its defining water tunnel feature.

Activities

Cave exploration, nature walks, small water passages.

Best Time to Visit

Dry season for safer cave entry (less risk of sudden water flow).

How to Get There

In Barangay Cumabao, Tumauini — accessible by local transport from Tumauini town.

Tips

Bring headlamps, sturdy shoes, and watch for slippery areas inside the cave.

Step into the cave, follow the water’s path, and uncover the hidden wonders of Kweba Danum.
