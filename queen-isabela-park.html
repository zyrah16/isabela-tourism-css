Queen Isabela Park:
A Tribute to Heritage and Identity

History

Queen Isabela Park in Alibagu, Ilagan City is named after Queen Isabella II of Spain, the namesake of Isabela province. The park features a central statue of the queen with surrounding open green space and markers honoring local leaders and history.

Activities

Leisurely walks, photography with the Queen Isabella statue, picnics, and small community gatherings.

Best Time to Visit

Late afternoon or early evening for cooler temperatures and soft light for photos.

How to Get There

The park is easily reached from Ilagan City center by tricycle or jeepney; it’s near the provincial capitol area.

Tips

Bring water and consider a light snack — parks often have limited food options nearby.

A relaxing cultural stop that celebrates provincial identity and offers peaceful reflection.
