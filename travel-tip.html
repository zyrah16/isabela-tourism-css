Plan Your Isabela Adventure

Essential tips, local insights, and handy advice for a smooth and fun visit.

Best Time to Visit

The dry season (November to May) is ideal for exploring Isabela’s waterfalls, mountains, and festivals. Bambanti Festival in January is a must-see, so plan around that if you want to experience the biggest event in the province.

How to Get There

Bus

Several bus companies from Manila, Tuguegarao, or nearby cities go to major towns like Ilagan, Cauayan, and Santiago.

Private Car

Isabela is accessible via the Maharlika Highway (Pan-Philippine Highway).

Air

The nearest airport is Cauayan Airport, ideal for faster access.

Getting Around the Province

Tricycles and jeepneys cover short distances in towns and small cities.

Motorbike or van rentals are ideal for exploring remote attractions like waterfalls or heritage sites.

Roads in rural areas can be rough; a vehicle with good ground clearance is recommended for adventure trips.

Where to Stay

Ilagan City

Several hotels and inns near city center—good base for sightseeing.

Cauayan City

Great if visiting Bambanti Festival or other northern attractions.

Homestays or eco-lodges

Perfect for a more authentic experience near natural attractions.

Food and Drinks

Try local specialties like Pinilisa, Big Brother Cornick, Mung Bean Products, Gin C, and fresh tilapia dishes.

Small eateries often have authentic dishes; don’t miss street food during festivals.

Always carry water and snacks when hiking or visiting remote waterfalls.

Things to Bring

Comfortable walking shoes and clothes suitable for outdoor activities.

Sunscreen, hat, and insect repellent—especially in nature or farm tours.

Camera or smartphone for the scenic spots.

Cash—many rural areas don’t accept cards.

Safety Tips

Stick to designated tourist paths, especially near rivers, waterfalls, and mountains.

Check weather updates before heading out; flash floods can occur in rainy months.

If you’re trekking or rafting, go with a local guide for safety and to learn more about the area.

Suggested Itineraries

1-Day Adventure

Visit Magat River, nearby waterfalls, and try local delicacies in town.

3-Day Festival Trip

Time it with Bambanti Festival, explore Ilagan City, and see local heritage sites.

Nature Escape

Spend 2–3 days in Palanan or other rural areas for waterfalls, trekking, and farm experiences.

Plan smart, travel safe, and make the most out of your Isabela adventure.
