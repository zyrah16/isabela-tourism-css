Mabbanga Festival

About

The Mabbanga Festival, celebrated every December 8 in Santa Maria, highlights the town’s Ibanag pottery tradition — a craft believed introduced by early indigenous artisans. It’s a week‑long event that honors culture, heritage, and the resilience of a community that shaped Santa Maria over generations.

Highlights

Cultural presentations, pottery exhibits, street parades, traditional music, and community gatherings showcase artistry and local customs.

Best Time to Visit

Early December, especially around the fiesta dates, when cultural displays and community celebrations are at their peak.

Tips

Plan ahead for December travel — holiday events may increase visitor numbers.

The Mabbanga Festival honors tradition, community heritage, and the enduring creative spirit of Santa Maria’s people.
