Abuan River:
Ride the Flow of Adventure

About

Abuan River, once labeled among the cleanest rivers in Cagayan Valley, winds through lush Sierra Madre forests. It’s now a burgeoning eco‑adventure destination with water activities.

Activities

Rafting, kayaking, boat rides, picnic spots, and adventure rides like banana boats and jet skis at the Abuan River Adventure & Eco Park.

Best Time to Visit

Dry season for safer water activities and clearer water.

How to Get There

Located in Ilagan City — accessible by road and via Ilagan City tourism services.

Tips

Facilities may vary, so check current offerings with local tourism before going.

Ride the waters, enjoy the views, and make unforgettable memories along Abuan River.
