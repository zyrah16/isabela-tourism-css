Dibulo Falls:
Chasing Hidden Waterfalls

About

Dibulo Falls is an iconic multi‑layered waterfall deep in the Sierra Madre range. It’s known for its cool mountain pool and pristine wilderness setting.

Activities

Trekking, swimming, picnics by the pool, waterfall appreciation.

Best Time to Visit

Dry season (December-May) — trail is easier to hike without mud or strong currents.

How to Get There

Reach Barangay Dibulo in Dinapigue by road then trek to the falls. Local guides improve safety and route finding.

Tips

Wear sturdy footwear; the terrain can be slippery near the falls.

Feel the cool mist, take in the sights, and let Dibulo Falls refresh your spirit.
